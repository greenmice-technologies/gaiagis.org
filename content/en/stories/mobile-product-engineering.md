## Problem

Mobile products in enterprise settings must stay consistent with evolving backends, identity models, and operational telemetry—without turning every release into a coordination crisis.

## Solution

We treat mobile engineering as part of the system: shared contracts with backend teams, release discipline, and observability that matches how products are actually supported in the field.

## Architecture

- Integration with enterprise identity, payments, and line-of-business APIs
- Offline-aware behavior and clear failure modes where connectivity is uneven
- Crash and performance analytics wired into engineering workflows—not vanity metrics

## Impact

- Coherent user experiences across platforms without sacrificing engineering standards
- Faster, safer releases through automation and shared ownership with backend services
