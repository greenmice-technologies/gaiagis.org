We engineer platforms and services that survive organizational change: explicit contracts, strong operational signals, and migration paths that reduce risk instead of hiding it.

### What we deliver

- Service boundaries and API design aligned to real team topology
- **Reliability engineering**: SLO thinking, incident workflows, and observability that matches on-call reality
- **Data movement** across systems with traceability and reconciliation
- **Security posture** aligned to enterprise standards—without “security theater”

### Where this shows up

Large enterprises, national-scale operators, and product companies that must integrate with legacy stacks and external partners without freezing innovation.

---

Modernization fails when it is treated as a one-shot rewrite. We focus on **incremental extraction**: reduce coupling, isolate domains, and validate behavior continuously.

### Methods we use

- Strangler patterns and anti-corruption layers
- Contract-first APIs and schema evolution discipline
- Automated testing at the seams: golden datasets, replay harnesses, and parity checks
- Operational cutovers planned with rollback paths

### Results

Lower risk per release, predictable costs, and teams that regain velocity without betting the business on a big-bang launch.
