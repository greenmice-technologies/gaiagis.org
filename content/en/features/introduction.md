Gaia is a map and charts CMS for teams that need to create, edit, organize, and publish geographic projects from the browser.
It is designed as a modern alternative to desktop GIS-heavy workflows: no client installation, collaborative by default, and built on open technologies.

Core product characteristics include:

- Browser-based editing for maps, layers, styles, and print layouts
- SaaS and self-hosted deployment options
- White-label support for organizational branding
- Interoperability with existing OGC and geospatial infrastructure



# Authentication

- Login username/email + password with JWT.
- Account activation, reset password, user profile.
- OAuth providers managed on backend and frontend.

# Backoffice and content

- CRUD administrative and content on users, profiles, settings, taxonomies and OAuth providers.
- CRUD application on projects, layers, styles, print layouts, assets, resources and projections.

# Geospatial capabilities

- Management of vector, raster, WMS and WFS layers.
- Import of geospatial files with support for OpenLayers and gdal3.js.
- External resources with browser capabilities.
- Styling with GeoStyler and conversion to SLD and QGIS/QML formats.
- Editing of geometries and attributes in the table.
- Export of cartographic data to GeoPDF.
