Gaia projects keep layers, styles, resources, and map views organized in a single browser workspace.

Project workflows include:

- Creating private projects for internal work
- Publishing public projects for external sharing
- Reusing project assets across cartographic workflows
- Collaborating with visibility and ownership boundaries
